Omlette

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious omlette.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon 35% heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs and heavy cream together in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Melt coconut oil in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture and cover skillet with a lid.
  3. Cook until eggs begin to set, about 5 minutes. Fold omelet in half; cover and continue cooking until firm, about 5 minutes more. Transfer to a plate using a flat spatula.
